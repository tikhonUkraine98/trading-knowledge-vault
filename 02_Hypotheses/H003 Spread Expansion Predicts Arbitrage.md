# H003 Spread Expansion Predicts Arbitrage

## Statement

Widening [[Bid-Ask Spread]] may increase the probability of future [[Cross Exchange Arbitrage]] opportunities.

## Mechanism

Bid-ask spread expands.

Liquidity weakens.

Price discovery becomes less stable.

Cross-exchange dislocation becomes more likely.

## Required data

- bid-ask spread history
- cross-exchange spread history
- order book depth
- trade frequency
- execution cost
