# H004 Range Bot Detection

## Statement

[[Range Bot]] behavior may be detectable through repeated market rhythm inside a narrow range.

## Possible signals

- stable price corridor
- repetitive trade sizes
- recurring buy and sell zones
- repeated tape rhythm
- refill behavior
- volume decay before shutdown

## Required data

- [[Order Book]]
- [[Tape]]
- spread
- price range
- trade size distribution
- cycle frequency

## Related concepts

- [[Range Bot]]
- [[Refill]]
- [[Tape]]
- [[Liquidity Wall]]
