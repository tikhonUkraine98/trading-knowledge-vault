# H002 Absorption Precedes Reversal

## Statement

[[Absorption]] may precede reversals or exhaustion.

## Simple meaning

If aggressive buyers attack but price does not rise, or sellers attack but price does not fall, opposing liquidity may be absorbing the pressure.

## Required data

- [[Tape]]
- [[Order Book]]
- [[Delta]]
- [[CVD]]
- price reaction after event

## Test method

Detect high aggressive volume with weak price continuation.

Then measure whether price reverses or stalls more often than random baseline.
