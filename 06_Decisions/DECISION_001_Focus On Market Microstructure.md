# DECISION_001 Focus On Market Microstructure

## Decision

The project should focus on measurable market microstructure instead of classical chart patterns.

## Previous framing

Find a profitable pattern.

## New framing

Find a measurable market property that creates stable inefficiency, validate execution, and only then evaluate edge.

## Not primary focus now

- pretty chart patterns
- candle formations
- secret indicators
- unfalsifiable narratives

## Primary focus now

- [[Order Book]]
- [[Tape]]
- [[Liquidity]]
- [[Bid-Ask Spread]]
- [[Order Flow]]
- [[Absorption]]
- [[Cross Exchange Arbitrage]]
- [[Range Bot]]

## Reason

The most promising opportunities currently appear to come from:

- structural exchange differences
- order book microstructure
- market maker behavior
- cross-exchange lag
- liquidity imbalance
- range-bot-like algorithms
