# ARB_021_MISPRICING_DETECTOR

## Purpose

Detect temporary mispricing between identical or strongly related assets.

## Mispricing locations

- between exchanges
- spot and derivatives
- related crypto assets
- related market sectors

## Inputs

- prices from multiple venues
- order book depth
- fees
- spread history
- execution feasibility

## Outputs

- mispricing event
- spread size
- estimated persistence
- feasibility score
