# Cross Exchange Arbitrage

## Ukrainian
Міжбіржовий арбітраж.

## Simple meaning
Купити актив на одній біржі дешевше і продати на іншій дорожче.

## Important correction

Real arbitrage is not just visible spread. It must pass execution validation.

## Real profit must account for

- cross-exchange spread
- [[Bid-Ask Spread]] on both exchanges
- fees
- slippage
- transfer cost
- transfer time
- transfer status
- inventory availability
- exchange reliability
